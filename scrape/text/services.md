1-hour Consult
A once-off, personalised sleep or parenting consult for families who want clarity, reassurance, and practical guidance without ongoing support. You’ll complete a brief intake ahead of time, followed by a 60-minute consult where we explore normal sleep/behaviour/development, routines, rhythms, and responsive strategies to support your baby’s sleep / development now and going forward. A written summary and relevant resources are included.
  [LINK -> https://www.beyondtheirdreams.com/hourly-calls] Tell me more
Tell me more
Prenatal Consult
A 60-min once off consult to discuss what you can realistically expect in the first months postpartum. We will go through normal infant sleep, temperament, sleep shaping, safe infant sleep, as well as what flags to look out for and much much more!
