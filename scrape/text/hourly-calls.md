## [H2] 1-hour Consult
1-hour Consult
What's the process?
Best suited for families who need clarity, reassurance, and practical guidance - not ongoing support.
This once-off consult is ideal if your baby’s sleep or behavioural challenges feel manageable but confusing, or if you want to sense-check what you’re doing and make small, realistic adjustments without committing to a longer support package.
