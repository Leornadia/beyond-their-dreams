## [H2] Why Baby-Led Sleep?
Why Baby-Led Sleep?
The Baby-Led Sleep™ approach will teach you how to connect with your baby on a deeper level and how to reinforce important feelings of safety and security so that when developmentally ready, your baby will be comfortable falling asleep.
The Baby-Led Sleep™ approach will help you learn about yourself as a parent and your impact on your baby’s sleep.
The Baby-Led Sleep™ approach will guide you to being a responsive parent by listening to your child’s cues and dealing with your child respectfully while supporting any distress, in an effort to build and nurture trust.
The Baby-Led Sleep™ approach will ensure you understand realistic, age-appropriate expectations about sleep.
