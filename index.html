About My Approach

Honouring the mother's instincts and the baby's biological cues.

The Baby-Led Sleep™ Philosophy

We are passionate about helping families improve sleep without resorting to sleep training.

The concept of "sleep training" goes against everything I believe in. It is critical to understand that babies who have been sleep trained have not been trained to sleep independently or connect sleep cycles; they have purely learned not to signal to their caregiver when they have needs. They have learned that when they cry, no one is coming.

Our approach respects and nurtures the attachment system. It supports the natural progression of infant development and gives parents a clear, evidence-based understanding of what "normal" baby sleep looks like. We appreciate your baby's still-developing brain and dominant survival instinct.

Respect the Attachment

Secure attachment is the primary foundation of emotional regulation and sleep readiness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Baby-Led Sleep and how virtual packages operate.

Are your services in person or virtual?

All services are 100% Virtual, with consultations completed via Zoom and follow-up contact either via email, WhatsApp, or additional Zoom calls as indicated.

A key element of my approach is to foster a close connection between parent and child, as well as to empower parents to build the confidence to follow their instincts. This is not possible with a stranger in your home telling you what to do in your most vulnerable and personal spaces. Virtual support ensures you retain sovereignty over your home while receiving expert advice.

What age babies do you work with?

I work with babies and toddlers from 0 – 36 months. Each age and stage comes with its own unique biological challenges, but my core approach never changes. It always remains fully tailored to each unique child, ensuring parents are empowered to remain responsive and connected to their children while accepting biological norms and maintaining realistic expectations.

Should my partner join the consultations?

It would be wonderful to have both caregivers participate in the consultations. This is a great way to ensure a team approach is taken and that everyone is on the same page when it comes to the responsive sleep road maps and loving boundaries put in place.

However, it is not essential if logistically it cannot be done. A detailed email summary of our calls will always be sent to you, which you can easily share with your partner.

Why don’t you use sleep training in your approach?

The concept of "sleep training" goes against everything I believe in. It is important to understand that babies who have been "sleep trained" have not been trained to sleep independently or connect sleep cycles—sleep is a biological need, not a skill we can teach.

These babies have purely learned not to signal to their caregiver when they have needs. They still wake up just as frequently as untrained babies, but their distress has been ignored so they remain quiet. Sleep training does not honour a parent's innate instinct to remain connected. Our responsive strategies support parents to get more sleep without using alarm-inducing separation.

How do we book a package with you?

You can book any service or schedule a free 15-minute clarity call directly on our site using the booking buttons, or send me an email directly at nicole@beyondtheirdreams.com. We will respond within 24 hours to align on your family's needs!
